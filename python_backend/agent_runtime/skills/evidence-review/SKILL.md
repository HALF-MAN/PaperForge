# Evidence Review

Use this skill after risk scoring.

## Instructions

- Review consistency across strategy, market data, backtest, and risk artifacts.
- Identify missing evidence or contradictions.
- Prepare the mission for paper simulation or human approval.
- Do not bypass a risk block.

## Deliverable

The deliverable is a `ReviewReport` artifact.
